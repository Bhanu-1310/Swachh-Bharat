About

"Cleanliness is next to godliness"

To accelerate the efforts to achieve universal
sanitation coverage and to put focus on sanitation,
the Prime Minister of India had launched the Swachh Bharat Mission on 2nd October 2014.Taking this initiative forward we have built this website to help in sanitation.

A person anywhere in the city sees junk/garbage piled up can register a complaint on this website.The person can add real time location and the problem on our website.

As soon as we recieve your complaint we send garbage truck/trolley on the site and clean it up.In this way we can both help our city remain clean and proper sanitation is maintained.We are working in cooperation with The Municipal Corporation/Council of every city.

This is the official site authorised by the government."Beware of fake websites"
